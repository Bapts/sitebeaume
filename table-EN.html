Our Table d'hote
Discover our specialties from Burgundy, that we make with love and with local ingredients or grown-in-our-yard fruits and vegetables!

Your journey in France and especially Burgundy wouldn't be complete without discovering our yummy specialties.

We would be happy to share that special moment with you (please reserve beforehand) around our big table d'hote, Burgundy delicatessen and products grown locally or even in our own yard.

Diner will be served with a Kir as an aperitif, as well as wine during the meal, moderately of course! At the end of diner, we'll be happy to provide tea, coffee or infusion.

Rate : 25€ per guest, 13€ for below 12-year-old guests!
  • Diner is served at 7:30pm
  • Please book your diner at least 24 hours in advance
  • Breakfast will be served in the garden or the living room
    depending on the weather, between 8:00 and 10:30
    If you are to leave earlier, please tell us
    in advance so that we can work it out!
