Location
Less than 5 minutes from the A6 motorway exit Pouilly-en-Auxois (interchange 28), follow Créancey at roundabout and take the D16 road (first on your left before Créancey), cross the village until Saint-Gervais chapel, access 50m after the turn on your left.
From Dijon take the A38 motorway, exit named Créancey. After the Beaume road sign, la Maison de Beaume is the second house on your right.


About us
La Maison de Beaume is an old farmhouse that became family property in 1975. It is on a nice 3000 m² (32,300 sq ft) land and is dated back at least 1840. Beaume is a small typical French village, with its farms, lots of flowers and nature, and all of its stone houses. It hosted up to 7 windmills, that are now gone but can still be imagined here and there in the peaceful French countryside landscape.
La Maison de Beaume is an old farmhouse built before 1840, that our family bought back in 1975. It sits between a farm and the peaceful creek in the back of the land (securized for children).
A lot of work was done on the property this last decades, and in 2018, we decided to open there a guesthouse with 4 rooms and everything to make you have nice holidays. This project is really important for us, all the more since we celebrated our wedding here in the house!
The discovery of France and Burgundy wouldn't ever be complete without good French food!
We would be happy to share that special moment with you (please reserve beforehand) around our big table d'hote, Burgundy delicatessen and products grown locally or even in our own yard.
